Khudkila Nepal

company aiming to work with students to change their learning experience

We are a team of engineers and educators bound together to change Nepal's practical, technical and implementable education system. We specialize in hardware and software designing, development, maker education, and consultancy services which are run by an experienced and specialized team working in the multiple dimensions of the community.We would like to be with other groups, institutions, and foundations that want to inspire students to pause and consider where and how they are going. To take the initiative in our approach to growth & development, and also the responsibility for such change, that we want to see in the world around us. Our vision is to recognize, cultivate, and promote the qualities of leadership, confidence, responsibility, teamwork, and a global perspective in students and young learners using creative and innovative teaching techniques as well as appropriate counseling and assistance.

What Our Agency Provides

  • Robotics Training

    Robotics training services aim to equip individuals and organizations with the skills and knowledge necessary to effectively utilize robotics technology.

    These services can include workshops, seminars, and hands-on training sessions to cover various aspects of robotics, from design and engineering to programming and implementation.

  • Drone Training

    Drone training services provide individuals and organizations with the knowledge and skills needed to safely and effectively operate unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs).

    These services can include theoretical and practical training, covering topics such as flight mechanics, navigation, regulations, and risk management to ensure responsible and competent drone operation.

  • 3d Designing Training

    3D printing training services aim to educate individuals and organizations on the use and applications of 3D printing technology.

    These services can include workshops, seminars, and hands-on training sessions that cover topics such as 3D modeling, software usage, and printing techniques, to ensure a comprehensive understanding of the technology and its capabilities.

  • Scratch Programming

    Scratch programming training services offer individuals, especially children and youth, the opportunity to learn the basics of computer programming through a visual and interactive platform.

    These services typically include workshops and classes that teach students how to use Scratch, a block-based programming language, to create games, animations, and interactive projects, while also developing problem-solving and critical thinking skills.
